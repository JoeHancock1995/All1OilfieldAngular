Inventory Assesment

Alliance One Oilfield provides inventory assessment survey. This can be performed on either capital equipment or spare inventory. We will perform a survey, whether on the rig or within a shore based warehouse to determine condition of equipment. Typically this service is performed on equipment that has been stacked and prior to being re-activated allowing the customer to know exact condition prior to the re-activation. Spare inventory assessment also looks at condition of spares and determines whether spares need to be replaced etc.
